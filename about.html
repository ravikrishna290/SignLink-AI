About SignLink AI

Bridging the communication gap with cutting-edge visual artificial intelligence.

Our Mission

SignLink AI is dedicated to making communication seamlessly accessible for everyone. We believe that technology should not isolate us, but instead serve as a powerful bridge connecting the Deaf and hard-of-hearing community with the rest of the world effortlessly.

The DeepBlue Project

Originally conceptualized as part of the rigorous DeepBlue ISL initiative, SignLink AI utilizes state-of-the-art Neural Networks to translate regional Indian Sign Language gestures in real-time. By processing rapid 3D spatial joint coordinates locally on your machine, we ensure maximum privacy and sub-second translation latency natively within any modern web browser.

The Future

Our commitment doesn't stop here. We are continuously expanding our machine-learning datasets to encompass diverse regional ISL dialects, complex sentence structures, and multi-handed tracking pipelines. The future of communication is barrier-free.
