How SignLink Works

A simple step-by-step guide explaining how our AI translates sign language in real-time.

1. Select Your Category

Choose the context of your conversation from our dropdown menu (e.g., Alphabets, Numbers, Workplaces). This allows our machine learning models to focus on the most relevant gestures for higher accuracy.

2. Start Your Camera

Click the 'Start Camera' button and ensure you are well-lit. Make sure your hands and upper body are clearly visible to the lens so the algorithm can track every micro-movement.

3. AI Landmark Detection

As you gesture, our backend securely processes the video stream frame by frame using Google's MediaPipe technology. We extract 21 precise 3D coordinate points from each hand.

4. Real-time Translation

These coordinate points are instantly fed into our custom-trained Neural Networks. Within milliseconds, the AI predicts the corresponding sign and updates the 'Translated Text' box on your screen!
